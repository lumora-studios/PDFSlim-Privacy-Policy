Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use PDFSlim ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Our primary goal is to provide a fast, secure, and fully private document experience. PDFSlim processes all your documents and files locally on your device. We do not upload your PDFs, images, or documents to any remote servers for processing.

Permissions Requested by the App:

  • Camera Access: We request camera permission solely to allow you to take photos for document scanning and image-to-PDF conversion. The captured images remain securely on your device.
  • Storage/Files Access: We require read and write access to your local storage to open, modify, save, and manage your PDF files.
  • Internet Access: Internet permission is used for providing digital advertisements through Google AdMob and for verifying premium purchases.

2. Third-Party Services

While our core PDF features function entirely offline, our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you to serve personalized content or process transactions.

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to serve personalized ads. View Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: If you choose to make in-app purchases or subscribe, payments are processed securely through Google Play. We do not process, collect, or have access to your raw payment details (such as your credit card number).

3. Data Security and Retention

Because your sensitive documents are processed locally, the security of those files relies on the security of your device. We strongly recommend maintaining standard device security measures such as screen locks. Your files are retained on your device until you choose to delete them.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
